Lukas Ligeti

Lukas is a composer and improvising drummer/ percussionist/ electronics performer. He is a core faculty member of the PhD program in Integrated Composition, Improvisation and Technology at the University of California, Irvine. Lukas divides his time between Irvine, California and Johannesburg, South Africa, and also spends significant time in New York City; Vienna, Austria; and Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso.
